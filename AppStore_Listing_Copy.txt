Full Description (paste into the Description field):
Timegrapher Logging is the essential tool for watchmakers and horologists who need precise, professional records of their timegrapher work.

This app is 100% free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Easily log rate, amplitude, and beat error across all standard and diagonal positions. Get instant calculations for averages, maximum delta, horizontal and vertical averages, and vertical drop.

Powerful Analysis Tools
• Dynamic Poising — Automatically identifies the fastest vertical position at low amplitude. Visualize the heavy spot on a detailed balance wheel graphic with crown orientation and expert guidelines.

• Isochronism Measurements — Record readings 24 hours later to calculate rate changes (Δ) per position. View color-coded results with clear guidelines for modern and vintage movements.

Professional Export
Generate publication-quality results images and save them directly to your camera roll. Perfect for client records, service reports, or your personal archives.

Additional features include custom lift angle and beat rate support, one-tap keyboard dismissal, and clean, efficient tables designed specifically for watchmakers.

Whether you're servicing modern movements or restoring vintage timepieces, Timegrapher Logging helps you work faster and document your work with confidence.
