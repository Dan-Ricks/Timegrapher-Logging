Timegrapher Logging - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

Timegrapher Logging does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app only requests permission to save images to your device's Photo Library when you choose to use the "Save Timegrapher results to camera roll" feature. These images are created from data you enter and are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any servers.

No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services are used.

Optional tips can be purchased via in-app purchase. No personal information is collected or stored for tips.

For questions, contact Dan Ricks.
